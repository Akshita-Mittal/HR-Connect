WHY HR Connect

We strongly believe that ‘your success is our success ‘ , thus we never tend to let you loose track of your success path, which in turn helps us also to retain on ours!

Established in 2015, HR Connect was created with a vision to fill the gaping disparity between the human resource requirement of the corporate world and the existing skilled manpower across various verticals. HR Connect has today developed into one of the leading consulting firms with services in various sectors of domestic markets.

We are Trusted Advisors to both our clients and our candidates. Our specialties include providing relevant talent solutions to a cross section of industries including the Information Technology, Electronics, Telecom, Manufacturing & Process , Pharmaceuticals, Engineering and the Services Industry.
